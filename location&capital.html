LOCATION & CAPITAL

Mexico is a country in the southern portion of North America, a federation comprising 31 states, including Mexico City (CDMX), which is the capital city and its largest metropolis.
Other major urban areas include Guadalajara (Chinese: 瓜達拉哈拉), Monterrey (Chinese: 蒙特雷), Puebla (Chinese: 普埃布拉), Toluca (Chinese: 托盧卡), Tijuana (Chinese: 提華納), Ciudad Juárez (Chinese: 華瑞茲城), and León (Chinese: 雷昂).

LOCATION

Mexico is located in the southern portion of North America, between the United States and Central America. Mexico is bordered by the U.S. to the north and Guatemala and Belize to the south. The entire western border of Mexico is a coastline of the Pacific Ocean, except for the inward jutting Gulf of California that separates the Baja Region with the rest of the country. On the eastern coastline of Mexico you will find the Gulf of Mexico.

CAPITAL

Mexico City (Chinese: 墨西哥城; Spanish: Ciudad de México) is geographically located in the Valley of Mexico. This valley, also known as the Valley of the Damned is a large valley in the high plateaus at the centre of Mexico. It has an altitude of 2,240 meters (7,349 ft). The Federal District of Mexico City is situated in central-south Mexico and it is surrounded by the state of Mexico on the west, north and east, and by the state of Morelos on the south. The city covers an area of around 1,485 sq km with the elevation of 2,240m (7,349 ft).
