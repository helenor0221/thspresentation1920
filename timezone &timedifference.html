TIME ZONE & TIME DIFFERENCE 

With its varying Daylight Saving Time (DST) schedules, Mexico's time zone situation frequently causes confusion. The country has 4 standard time zones, which mirror the time zones in the contiguous United States. There are 3 corresponding DST time zones. The time difference with Hong Kong is 13 hours.
