POPULATION

The current population of Mexico is 128,766,554 as of Monday, May 18, 2020, based on Worldometer elaboration of the latest United Nations data.

Mexico ranks number 10 in the list of countries (and dependencies) by population.

Mexico population is equivalent to 1.65% of the total world population.

The population density in Mexico is 66 per km^2 (172 people per mi^2).

The median age in Mexico is 29.2 years.
