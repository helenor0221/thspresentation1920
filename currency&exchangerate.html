CURRENCY

The Mexican peso (sign: $; code: MXN) is the currency of Mexico. Modern peso and dollar currencies have a common origin in the 15th–19th century Spanish dollar, most continuing to use its sign, "$". The Mexican peso is the 10th most traded currency in the world, the third most traded currency from the Americas (after the United States dollar and Canadian dollar), and the most traded currency from Latin America. There are currently 5, 10, 20, 50 cents and 1, 2, 5, 10, 20 pesos coins; 20, 50, 100, 200, 500, 1000 pesos banknotes.
